The Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Wimberly Chamber of Commerce

I chose Wimberly Chamber of Commerce because Wimberly is a town not too far where I live in Texas. It is little but so much goes on there and I think it would be a good place to do this assignment for.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to be a central hub of information about Wimberly and the businesses in that area. It's a good place to get your business promoted so that all can see and be involved in it also.

Scenarios

-What are good places to eat in Wimberly?

-What activities are there to experince there?

-Is this town a good place to start a business and what types of businesses are already there?

Color Schema

It is a little town in Texas that is very secluded that feels very friendly and inviting. I want there to be duller colors as it blends in with the surroundings of the town. I was thinking tan for the background of the pages with some blues and greens. I want there to be dark words for the information on the pages to help brighten the page. I think I want the heading at the top a cream color, the nav bar to be blue, and the background to be tan. I will implement a dark green in there to help with the light and darks contrast.

Typography

The fonts I have chosen to use is Open Sans for the simplicity of it. I also want to in others for things such as titles and headers to add a little more detail. I have not come up with one to use but I will add it when I do.
